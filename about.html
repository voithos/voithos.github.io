Hi there! I’m Zaven.

I’m a software developer living in the pleasant Tri-Cities, WA with my wonderful wife Daisy.

This space is where I (aspire to) write about the things that I learn or find thought-provoking. Hence, the name of the site – “skepsi” is the Greek word for “thought.”

I find most things mathematical, scientific, or technological to be highly interesting, but I am particularly fascinated (enthralled, even) by computer science and programming. I enjoy learning about various such topics, and building interesting things based on what I learn.

If, perchance, anything here is interesting or useful to you, then feel free to copy, share, or extend it – the content is all under CC-BY.

I use the name "voithos" on most sites. If you’d like, you can send me an email or tweet at @zvoithos. You can also check out some of my projects on GitHub.

Thanks for reading, and have a nice day!
